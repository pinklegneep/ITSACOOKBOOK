Protein Waffles

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
3 mins 12 mins 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 125 g All Purpose Flour
  • 80 ml Protein Powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 12g Sugar
  • 12g Baking Power
  • 350ml Milk
  • 30ml Oil

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, add flour, protein powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Whisk until evenly combined
  2. Add milk, apple cider vinegar and oil
  3. Whisk until batter is smooth and lightly thick
  4. Warm a waffle iron and grease with a pastry brush dipped in oil
  5. When warm, fill each waffle iron with about 1/3 cup batter
  6. Close the lid of the waffle maker and cook for 3-4 minutes
