Chicken Seitan

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
15 mins 30 mins 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 360g vital wheat gluten
  • 84g all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 710 ml broth
  • 4 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the vital wheat gluten, flour, nutritional yeast, onion powder, garlic powder, paprika and salt. Stir to combine.
  2. To the bowl with the dry ingredients, pour in the broth and oil. Stir well until the mixture starts to pull away from the bowl.
  3. Transfer to a lightly floured surface, and knead with your hands for just a minute until it comes together, not too much. It will still be quite messy, not a smooth dough.
