Buffalo Cauliflower

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 mins 15 mins 20 mins

Ingredients

  • ½ head cauliflower
  • ½ cup buffalo sauce
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • ½ tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Cut cauliflower into bite-sized florets. In a large bowl, gently stir together cauliflower and all remaining ingredients.
  2. Lightly grease your air fryer basket or rack. Arrange cauliflower in a single layer (working in batches if they don't all fit in a single layer). Cook at 375°F (190°C) for 12 to 15 minutes, or until fork tender and slightly browned.
  3. Serve warm with your favorite dipping sauce and celery sticks.
  4. To cook this in an oven, bake in a single layer at 450°F (230°C) for about 25 minutes.
