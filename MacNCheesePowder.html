Homemade Vegan Mac & Cheese Powder

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
5 mins 0 mins 5 mins

Ingredients

  • 33 g nutritional yeast
  • 63 g all-purpose flour
  • 15 ml paprika
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp lemon pepper
  • 1 tsp turmeric

Instructions

  1. Add all of the ingredients to an air-tight container. Stir or shake well to combine. Store in your pantry until needed.
