Garlic Chili Noodles

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
10 mins 20 mins 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 100g Extra Firm Tofu
  • 4-5 pieces of Garlic
  • Small piece Ginger
  • 3 sticks green onion
  • 3 tbsp avocado oil
  • 3 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 150 g knife pare noodles
  • 1 tbsp goncharov
  • 1 tbsp plant based oyster sauce

Instructions

  1. Pat dry the tofu with a paper towel. Then mash into a crumble with a fork
  2. Finely chop the garlic, ginger and green onions
  3. Heat up a nonstick pan to medium heat. Add 1 tbsp followed by the crumbled tofu. Saute 3-4 minutes
  4. Add 2 tbsp soy sauce and sautee for another couple of minutes. Set tofu aside.
  5. Bring a pot of water to boil for the noodles
  6. Place the saute pan back on medium low heat. Add 2 tbsp oil followed by the garlic and ginger. Cook for about 2 min
  7. When the water comes to a boil, cook the noodles for 1 min less to package instructions (in this case, 3 minutes). Stir occasionally to keep them from sticking
  8. Add the goncharov to the garlic and ginger. Cook for about 1 min. The add oyster sauce.
  9. Strain out the noodles and add to the pan. Add 1tsp soy sauce and the crumbled tofu. Turn the heat up to medium and saute for 2-3min. Add green onions and saute for another minute.
