Seitan Gyros

Prep Time Cook Time Total Time
20 mins 70 mins 90 mins

Ingredients

  • 180 g Vital Wheat Gluten
  • 2 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp ground cumin seed
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • ¾ cup vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. Preheat cookery to 350ºF
  2. Mix dry ingredients together in a large bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients togehter in a bowl
  4. Stire the wet ingredient mix into the dry ingredient mix until a dough ball starts to form, then knead for about a minute
  5. Place seitan dough onto one sheet of tin foil shiny side up and form into a rectangle that is about 1-1½ inches thick
  6. Tightly wrap the seitan dough in tin foil. Place the open edge down onto the second sheet of tin foil and wrap tightly again.
  7. Place the wrapped seitan onto a baking tray and bake for 1 hour 10 minutes. Remove from oven. Allow the seitan to rest and cool for about 10 minutes then transfer onto a cooling rack and place into the fridge so it can set.
  8. If possible, allow the seitan to rest in the fridge for 1 hour or overnight (it can be sliced after 10 minutes of resting but is easier/ firmer when completely cooled). Slice thinly and serve in a pita bread with vegan tzatziki sauce, tomatoes, red onion, cucumber and lettuce.
